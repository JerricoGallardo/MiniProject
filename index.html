Healthy Mouths, Healthier Minds
Bridging Oral Health and Mental Well-being

Are you aware that dental issues can negatively impact a person's social interactions and overall mental well-being? Oral health and mental health share a bidirectional relationship where poor oral hygiene can contribute to mental problems such as depression and anxiety, and vice versa.

On World Oral Health Day, we would like you to join us for this one-time event where we promote oral care to improve mental well-being.
